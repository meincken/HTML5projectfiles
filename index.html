About

AR Tuition is a tutoring business run by myself, Angela Rowland. I am an experienced primary school teacher with over 21 years of teaching experience and have taught in a variety of schools in Thurrock, Essex and Havering.

My particular expertise are in Key Stage Two English and Maths. Although I have taught across the primary age range, I have spent ten years teaching year 6 pupils, preparing them for their SATs.

As well as teaching in schools, I have been tutoring children since 1999. In that time, I have tutored children with different needs: children who struggle in Literacy, children who struggle in Maths, children who wish to sit their 11+ entrance exams, children who are taking the Brentwood School exam.

Whatever the needs of the child, tutoring helps to give him or her confidence and self-belief. This in turn, helps a child to flourish and succeed. This is why tutoring is such a rewarding job!

Currently, I tutor children in a home- environment in central Brentwood with no more than six pupils to a group. Children are grouped in similar ability groups. If you are interested in finding out more, please register your details to book a free assessment for your child.

Classes:

Primary English

Since the implementation of the new National Curriculum in primary schools, expectations on what children should achieve in English have significantly increased. There has also been a stronger emphasis on spelling, grammar and punctuation with children learning grammatical terminology which the majority of parents never learned at school.

  • Is your child struggling with reading or writing?
  • A R Tuition can help your child to gain confidence in:
  • Reading comprehension
  • Phonetics/ spellings
  • Writing skills

Primary Maths

If your child is struggling with Maths, tutoring can help to fill in any gaps in their learning and build on their knowledge and skills to become confident and capable mathematicians.

All groups are based on similar age and ability so that content is linked to the National Curriculum, reinforcing what they are learning at school.

  • Tuition will cover:
    • Place value and knowledge of number
    • Four rules- addition, subtraction, multiplication and division
    • Problem-solving
    • Fractions, decimals and percentages
    • Units of measurement- time, length, mass, volume
    • Data-handling
    • Shapes
